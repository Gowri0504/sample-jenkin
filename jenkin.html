Our Story

Founded in 2010, Savoria began with a simple belief: food should be an experience. Our chefs travel the world to bring you authentic flavours, using only the finest organic ingredients.

Every dish is crafted with passion, served in an atmosphere of understated elegance. We don’t just cook — we create memories.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?
Absolutely. We offer vegan, gluten‑free, and allergy‑friendly menus. Please inform your server.
What is your dress code?
Smart casual — we recommend collared shirts and closed shoes, but no strict dress code.
Do you take walk‑ins?
Yes, but reservations are highly recommended, especially on weekends.
Can I book a private event?
Certainly. Our private dining room seats up to 40 guests. Contact us for custom menus.
Do you offer gift cards?
Yes, available in any denomination, both digital and physical.
